<h3><strong>Our Ambitions</strong></h3>
<p>The Sentosa community will focus on six key areas to achieve the island's
dual goals of achieving carbon neutrality by 2030 and becoming a globally
recognised, certified sustainable tourism destination.</p>
<p>In 2022, SDC was conferred the Global Sustainable Tourism Council – For
Destinations (GSTC-D) certificate, affirming the organisation’s strong
push for collective and collaborative whole-of-island efforts towards achieving
a more sustainable Sentosa.</p>
