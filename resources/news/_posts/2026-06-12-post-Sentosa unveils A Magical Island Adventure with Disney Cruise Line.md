<p><strong>SINGAPORE, 12 June 2026 – </strong>Sentosa Development Corporation
(SDC) today announced a collaboration with Disney Cruise Line with the
launch of a three-month island-wide summer festivities titled <strong>A</strong>  <strong>Magical Island Adventure: Let’s Set Sail with Disney Cruise Line. </strong>This
is the first-of-its-kind large scale transformation of Sentosa where beloved
Disney stories and characters inspired by the <em>Disney Adventure’s</em> seven
themed areas will be featured all across the island’s precincts, landscapes
and natural character.<strong> </strong>Activities will run daily<strong> </strong>from
24 June to 20 September 2026, inviting guests to create their own unique
exploratory destination around Sentosa. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Designed as a day-to-night experience, <strong>A Magical Island Adventure</strong> combines
light, sound, movement and environment across Sentosa's precincts and natural
landscapes, from sunlit beach encounters and interactive play spaces to
glowing night-time environments and discovery-led trails. The island will
transform after dark where multiple glowing installations will light up
the spaces in vibrant colours. Also, for the first time, the Southernmost
Point of Continental Asia will extend its opening hours until 10.00pm (SGT)
as part of a dedicated night-time programme. The iconic location offers
guests a unique vantage point to experience Sentosa after dark.</p>

<p><strong>One Island, Seven Special Experiences</strong>
</p>
<p>Visitors can embark on a discovery trail at their own pace throughout
the day and night with an event passport as they explore different experiences
that span across the Singapore Cable Car - Sentosa Line to Sensoryscape,
Central Beach Bazaar, Palawan Beach, The Palawan, and the Southernmost
Point of Continental Asia. Each location will be decked out with features
that are inspired by the <em>Disney Adventure’s</em> iconic seven themed
areas such as the Disney Imagination Garden, Disney Discovery Reef, Town
Square, Wayfinder Bay, San Fransokyo Street, Toy Story Place, and Marvel
Landing. At each stop, visitors can collect a special medallion stamp in
their event passport that represents these onboard spaces.</p>
<p><u>Sentosa Express</u>
</p>
<p>The adventure begins before guests even set foot on the island. Starting
from early July, the Sentosa Express, Sentosa's iconic monorail, arrives
dressed for the occasion. Featuring Captain Mickey Mouse and Captain Minnie
Mouse on the train’s exterior, it welcomes guests into A Magical Island
Adventure from the moment they board at VivoCity, serving as a moving landmark
and a taste of the Disney Cruise Line magic that awaits.</p>
<p><u>Singapore Cable Car - Sentosa Line</u>
</p>
<p>A Magical Island Adventure will also take to the skies aboard the Singapore
Cable Car - Sentosa Line, extending the island's storytelling into the
air. All three stations — Sensoryscape, Imbiah Lookout, and Siloso Point
— will feature photo installations featuring various Disney characters,
ensuring the experience begins well before guests step into a cable car
cabin. The cabins will take on a new look with designs inspired by Disney
Cruise Line themed areas aboard the <em>Disney Adventure</em>, including
Toy Story Place and Disney Discovery Reef.&nbsp; From beach to skyline,
A Magical Island Adventure reimagines the Singapore Cable Car as an experience
that is visually immersive and full of discovery.</p>
<p><u>Southernmost Point of Continental Asia</u>
</p>
<p>One of Sentosa's most celebrated landmarks, the Southernmost Point of
Continental Asia, will transform into a luminous coastal escape drawing
inspiration from Disney Discovery Reef and Wayfinder Bay, bringing to life
Disney and Pixar stories like Finding Nemo, The Little Mermaid and Lilo
&amp; Stitch. The themed showpieces will cast the area in shifting oceanic
hues, while glowing manta ray installations illuminate the waters surrounding
the Southernmost Point bridge, creating a special after-dark atmosphere.</p>
<p>The Wayfinder Bay-inspired area further draws on the spirit of ocean voyaging
from the adventurous world of Walt Disney Animation Studio’s Moana, animating
the islet with wayfinding motifs, compass-inspired imagery and star-lit
pathways designed to reward exploration and discovery.</p>
<p>A dedicated ticket covers access to a unique night experience which offers
a gamified exploration journey, including a complimentary activity card
where guests can collect four unique embossed stamps, and a limited-edition <em>Disney Adventure-</em>themed
postcard keepsake. For ticket prices and bundle options, refer to Annex
A.</p>
<p><u>Palawan Beach</u>
</p>
<p>Palawan Beach serves as the heart of the island-wide activities where
visitors will get to enjoy installations themed after San Fransokyo Street,
Toy Story Place, and Marvel Landing at The Palawan all along Sentosa's
most beloved shoreline. Upon arrival at Palawan Beach, guests will be welcomed
by a 6-metre inflatable anchor installation flanked by sand-blasted figurines
of Captain Mickey Mouse and Captain Minnie Mouse, serving as the event's
signature outdoor photo moment.</p>
<p>The San Fransokyo Street themed area of the beach draws guests into the
eclectic world inspired by Walt Disney Animation Studio’s “Big Hero 6”
with a 6-metre Baymax inflatable and beachside deck chairs, creating a
relaxed atmosphere for visitors to linger and explore. A dedicated food
and retail precinct sits alongside, bringing together food trucks and retailers
within a lively beachside activation space, with offerings refreshed across
the three-month event period.</p>
<p>A short walk away, the shore features larger-than-life bath toys inspired
by Toy Story Place with fan-favourite characters like Woody, Buzz Lightyear,
Jessie and Bullseye, alongside bubble inflatables and a Toy Story splash
pad-inspired shoreline play area centred around a 3.9-metre shower-head
feature, with neon lighting transforming the space after dark.</p>
<p>At The Palawan @ Sentosa, guests will be treated to installations that
take their cue from Marvel Landing’s attractions like Ironcycle Test Run
roller coaster and Groot Galaxy Spin, alongside an Infinity Bar-inspired
space that will have visitors delighted at neon-lit cocktail sculptures
when the sun sets.</p>
<p><u>Central Beach Bazaar and Sensoryscape</u>
</p>
<p>A natural crossroads on Sentosa's beaches, Central Beach Bazaar is where
visitors are able to spot the 3.5-metre Magical Compass installation depicting
the <em>Disney Adventure’s</em> seven themed areas that also serve as a storytelling
guide across the island.</p>
<p>At Sensoryscape, the entire venue is transformed into a Town Square inspired
royal promenade lined with 13 princess-themed hammock installations spotlighting
beloved princesses and heroines like Ariel, Belle, Cinderella, Merida,
Mulan, Raya, Snow White, and more. Be sure to strike a pose with your favourite
Disney princess as you identify each of them only through their silhouettes
accompanied by recognisable motifs. Lighting plays a transformative role
in this space – shifting from warm, elegant tones, to kaleidoscopic colour
sequences that feature over 100 individual light effects to create a distinct
night-time experience – a reminder that on Sentosa, the adventure continues
even after sundown.</p>
<p>Within the same space, Tactile Trellis reimagines Disney Imagination Garden
through a five-metre topiary installation shaped after the Disney Cruise
Line logo, set among manicured greenery and floral displays.</p>
