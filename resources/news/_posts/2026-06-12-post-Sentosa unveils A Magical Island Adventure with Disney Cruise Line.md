<p><strong>SINGAPORE, 12 June 2026 – </strong>Sentosa Development Corporation
(SDC) today announced a collaboration with Disney Cruise Line with the
launch of a three-month island-wide summer festivities titled <strong>A</strong>  <strong>Magical Island Adventure: Let’s Set Sail with Disney Cruise Line. </strong>This
is the first-of-its-kind large scale transformation of Sentosa where beloved
Disney stories and characters inspired by the <em>Disney Adventure’s</em> seven
themed areas will be featured all across the island’s precincts, landscapes
and natural character.<strong> </strong>Activities will run daily<strong> </strong>from
24 June to 20 September 2026, inviting guests to create their own unique
exploratory destination around Sentosa. &nbsp;</p>
<p></p>
<p>Designed as a day-to-night experience, <strong>A Magical Island Adventure</strong> combines
light, sound, movement and environment across Sentosa's precincts and natural
landscapes, from sunlit beach encounters and interactive play spaces to
glowing night-time environments and discovery-led trails. The island will
transform after dark where multiple glowing installations will light up
the spaces in vibrant colours. Also, for the first time, the Southernmost
Point of Continental Asia will extend its opening hours until 10.00pm (SGT)
as part of a dedicated night-time programme. The iconic location offers
guests a unique vantage point to experience Sentosa after dark.</p>

<h4><strong>One Island, Seven Special Experiences</strong></h4>
<p>Visitors can embark on a discovery trail at their own pace throughout
the day and night with an event passport as they explore different experiences
that span across the Singapore Cable Car - Sentosa Line to Sensoryscape,
Central Beach Bazaar, Palawan Beach, The Palawan @ Sentosa, and the Southernmost
Point of Continental Asia. Each location will be decked out with features
that are inspired by the <em>Disney Adventure’s</em> iconic seven themed
areas such as the Disney Imagination Garden, Disney Discovery Reef, Town
Square, Wayfinder Bay, San Fransokyo Street, Toy Story Place, and Marvel
Landing. At each stop, visitors can collect a special medallion stamp in
their event passport that represents these onboard spaces.</p>
<p><strong><u>Sentosa Express</u></strong>
</p>
<p>The adventure begins before guests even set foot on the island. Starting
from early July, the Sentosa Express, Sentosa's iconic monorail, arrives
dressed for the occasion. Featuring Captain Mickey Mouse and Captain Minnie
Mouse on the train’s exterior, it welcomes guests into A Magical Island
Adventure from the moment they board at VivoCity, serving as a moving landmark
and a taste of the Disney Cruise Line magic that awaits.</p>
<p><strong><u>Singapore Cable Car - Sentosa Line</u></strong>
</p>
<p>A Magical Island Adventure will also take to the skies aboard the Singapore
Cable Car - Sentosa Line, extending the island's storytelling into the
air. All three stations — Sentosa Sensoryscape, Imbiah Lookout, and Siloso
Point — will feature photo installations featuring various Disney characters,
ensuring the experience begins well before guests step into a cable car
cabin. The cabins will take on a new look with designs inspired by Disney
Cruise Line themed areas aboard the <em>Disney Adventure</em>, including
Toy Story Place and Disney Discovery Reef.&nbsp; From beach to skyline,
A Magical Island Adventure reimagines the Singapore Cable Car as an experience
that is visually immersive and full of discovery.</p>
<p><strong><u>Southernmost Point of Continental Asia</u></strong>
</p>
<p>One of Sentosa's most celebrated landmarks, the Southernmost Point of
Continental Asia, will transform into a luminous coastal escape drawing
inspiration from Disney Discovery Reef and Wayfinder Bay, bringing to life
Disney and Pixar stories like Finding Nemo, The Little Mermaid and Lilo
&amp; Stitch. The themed showpieces will cast the area in shifting oceanic
hues, while glowing manta ray installations illuminate the waters surrounding
the Southernmost Point bridge, creating a special after-dark atmosphere.</p>
<p>The Wayfinder Bay-inspired area further draws on the spirit of ocean voyaging
from the adventurous world of Walt Disney Animation Studio’s Moana, animating
the islet with wayfinding motifs, compass-inspired imagery and star-lit
pathways designed to reward exploration and discovery.</p>
<p>A dedicated ticket covers access to a unique night experience which offers
a gamified exploration journey, including a complimentary activity card
where guests can collect four unique embossed stamps, and a limited-edition <em>Disney Adventure-</em>themed
postcard keepsake. For ticket prices and bundle options, refer to Annex
A.</p>
<p><strong><u>Palawan Beach</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Palawan Beach serves as the heart of the island-wide activities where
visitors will get to enjoy installations themed after San Fransokyo Street,
Toy Story Place, and Marvel Landing at The Palawan @ Sentosa all along
Sentosa's most beloved shoreline. Upon arrival at Palawan Beach, guests
will be welcomed by a 6-metre inflatable anchor installation flanked by
sand-blasted figurines of Captain Mickey Mouse and Captain Minnie Mouse,
serving as the event's signature outdoor photo moment.</p>
<p>The San Fransokyo Street themed area of the beach draws guests into the
eclectic world inspired by Walt Disney Animation Studio’s “Big Hero 6”
with a 6-metre Baymax inflatable and beachside deck chairs, creating a
relaxed atmosphere for visitors to linger and explore. A dedicated food
and retail precinct sits alongside, bringing together food trucks and retailers
within a lively beachside activation space, with offerings refreshed across
the three-month event period.</p>
<p>A short walk away, the shore features larger-than-life bath toys inspired
by Toy Story Place with fan-favourite characters like Woody, Buzz Lightyear,
Jessie and Bullseye, alongside bubble inflatables and a Toy Story splash
pad-inspired shoreline play area centred around a 3.9-metre shower-head
feature, with neon lighting transforming the space after dark.</p>
<p>At The Palawan @ Sentosa, guests will be treated to installations that
take their cue from Marvel Landing’s attractions like Ironcycle Test Run
roller coaster and Groot Galaxy Spin, alongside an Infinity Bar-inspired
space that will have visitors delighted at neon-lit cocktail sculptures
when the sun sets.</p>
<p><strong><u>Central Beach Bazaar and Sensoryscape</u></strong>
</p>
<p>A natural crossroads on Sentosa's beaches, Central Beach Bazaar is where
visitors are able to spot the 3.5-metre Magical Compass installation depicting
the <em>Disney Adventure’s</em> seven themed areas that also serve as a storytelling
guide across the island.</p>
<p>At Sentosa Sensoryscape, the entire venue is transformed into a Town Square
inspired royal promenade lined with 13 princess-themed hammock installations
spotlighting beloved princesses and heroines like Ariel, Belle, Cinderella,
Merida, Mulan, Raya, Snow White, and more. Be sure to strike a pose with
your favourite Disney princess as you identify each of them only through
their silhouettes accompanied by recognisable motifs. Lighting plays a
transformative role in this space – shifting from warm, elegant tones,
to kaleidoscopic colour sequences that feature over 100 individual light
effects to create a distinct night-time experience – a reminder that on
Sentosa, the adventure continues even after sundown.</p>
<p>Within the same space, Tactile Trellis at Sentosa Sensoryscape reimagines
Disney Imagination Garden through a five-metre topiary installation shaped
after the Disney Cruise Line logo, set among manicured greenery and floral
displays.</p>
<h4><strong>An Island Transformed After Dark</strong></h4>
<p>A Magical Island Adventure unfolds throughout the day, showcasing Sentosa’s
gradual transformation and inviting visitors to stay into the night, where
the island is reimagined through a captivating new perspective shaped by
luminous, neon-inspired lighting.</p>
<p>Ms Thien Kwee Eng, Chief Executive Officer of Sentosa Development Corporation
(SDC) said, "A Magical Island Adventure brings forth a new chapter for
Sentosa, one that shows the endless possibilities when world-class collaborations
are built around a strong narrative and a shared vision. By teaming up
with Disney Cruise Line, we are creating a first-of-its-kind day-to-night
outdoor journey that is emotionally stimulating and rewards exploration.
Whether you are a family seeking shared moments of wonder, a couple looking
to create memories together, or a visitor discovering Sentosa for the first
time, there is something here that will make you want to stay, explore,
and come back for more."</p>
<p>Ms Sarah Fox, Vice President and Regional General Manager, Southeast Asia
of Disney Cruise Line added, "Our collaboration with Sentosa extends the
Disney Cruise Line experience beyond the ship to Singapore’s most iconic
beach destination. This island-wide summer celebration brings more than
100 years of storytelling from Disney, Pixar and Marvel to life across
Sentosa, creating a distinctive destination experience for guests of all
ages and adds to the appeal and taste of a cruise vacation aboard the <em>Disney Adventure</em>."</p>
<p>Ms Jean Ng, Assistant Chief Executive, Experience Development Group, Singapore
Tourism Board, said, “A Magical Island Adventure is a wonderful addition
to Singapore's vibrant calendar of family-friendly adventures this summer.
This event transforms Sentosa with unique day-to-night programming, including
the immersive after-dark light installations at the Southernmost Point
of Continental Asia, allowing families fresh and imaginative ways to experience
the island and discover the magic of Disney Cruise Line. Collaborations
of this nature position Singapore as a hub for dynamic, world-class events
that deliver lasting memories for visitors of all ages. We invite families
to be part of the excitement at Sentosa this summer.”</p>
<h4><strong>Take a Piece of the Magical Island Home</strong></h4>
<p>The adventure extends into a specially created summer-style merchandise
range developed in collaboration with Disney Cruise Line, a collection
that feels right at home against Sentosa's sun, sand, and sea. The collection
includes an exclusively customised Sentosa and Disney Cruise Line-themed
tumbler, eco bag, towel, keychain, each piece designed to serve as a lasting
keepsake of the island experience. UOB cardholders can unlock a customised
blind pack Glow-in-the-Dark Medallion Pin with a minimum spend of $60,
more details can be found in Annex A.</p>
<h4><strong>Plan your visit</strong></h4>
<p>This three-month summer activation<em> </em>marks the beginning of an
evolving annual platform for Sentosa, with future editions to come. Across
the event period, select installations will be refreshed and fringe programming
introduced, giving guests a reason to return and discover something new
with every visit. Each year will bring a new story, a new collaboration,
and a new lens through which to experience the island, cementing Sentosa's
position as a destination that continually reimagines what a visit can
be.</p>

<p><strong>About Sentosa</strong>
</p>
<p>Sentosa, where discovery never ends, is Asia’s leading leisure destination
and Singapore’s premier island resort getaway, located within 15 minutes
from the central business and shopping districts. The island resort is
managed by Sentosa Development Corporation, which works with various stakeholders
in overseeing property investments, attractions development, and operation
of the various leisure offerings and management of the residential precinct
on the island.</p>
<p>The 500-hectare island resort is home to an exciting array of themed attractions,
award-winning spa retreats, lush rainforests, golden sandy beaches, resort
accommodations, world-renowned golf courses, a deep-water yachting marina
and luxurious residences – making Sentosa a vibrant island resort for business
and leisure. Sentosa is also home to Singapore’s first integrated resort,
Resorts World Sentosa, which operates Southeast Asia’s first Universal
Studios theme park.</p>
<p>Situated on the eastern end of Sentosa Island is Sentosa Cove, an exclusive
waterfront residential enclave bustling with more than 2,000 homes, quayside
restaurants, retail and specialty shops. The island is also proud to be
home to Sentosa Golf Club and its two acclaimed golf courses, The Serapong
and The Tanjong. Sentosa Golf Club has hosted a number of high-profile
professional and amateur tournaments, including the Singapore Open and
HSBC Women’s World Championship, welcoming international star players and
world-class golf professionals from across the world.</p>
<p>Welcoming a growing number of local and international guests every year,
Sentosa is an integral part of Singapore’s goal to be a global destination
to work, live and play. For more information, please visit <a href="http://www.sentosa.com.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">www.sentosa.com.sg</a>.</p>

<p><strong>About Sentosa Development Corporation</strong>
</p>
<p>Sentosa Development Corporation (SDC) was established on 1 September 1972
as a Statutory Board under the Ministry of Trade and Industry. As a master
planner, its charter since inception has been to oversee the development,
management, marketing and promotion of the island of Sentosa as a resort
destination for locals and tourists.</p>
<p>SDC wholly owns its subsidiaries Sentosa Cove Resort Management Pte Ltd
and Sentosa Golf Club. SDC also owns the Singapore Cable Car Sky Network,
managed by Mount Faber Leisure Group Pte Ltd, a wholly owned subsidiary
which operates as an autonomous commercial arm.</p>
<p>As a testament to its commitment to sustainability, SDC has been conferred
the Global Sustainable Tourism Council – For Destinations (GSTC-D) certificate,
making Sentosa the first island destination in Asia to receive this accolade.</p>
